ARTIST: Sunnyland Slim

PROFILE:
Blues singer and pianist, active from the 1920's through the 1980's, mainly in Chicago.
Born September 5, 1906 in Vance, Mississippi. Died March 17, 1995 in Chicago, Illinois.
