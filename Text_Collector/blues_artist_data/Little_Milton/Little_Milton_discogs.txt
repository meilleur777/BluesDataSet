ARTIST: Little Milton

PROFILE:
R&b singer, guitarist and songwriter, born 7 September 1934 in Inverness, Mississippi, died 4 August 2005 in Memphis, Tennessee.

Little Milton had a career that spanned from the mid-1950s up until shortly before his death in 2005, recording for labels such as [l=Sun Record Company], [l=Chess], [l=Stax] and [l=Malaco Records].

He legally dropped "James" as a first name after learning of a half-brother with the same name.
