ARTIST: Mick Taylor

PROFILE:
English guitarist, best known as a member of [a=John Mayall & The Bluesbreakers] (1966–69) and [a=The Rolling Stones] (1969–74). Born 17 January 1949 in Welwyn Garden City, Hertfordshire, England, UK.
Father of [a=Chloe Rose Taylor] (with his then girlfriend Rose Millar).

[b]For the songwriter of the [a=Marianne Faithfull] song "Cockleshells"/"Coquillages" please use [a=Michael John Taylor] (with ANV, if needed)[/b].
