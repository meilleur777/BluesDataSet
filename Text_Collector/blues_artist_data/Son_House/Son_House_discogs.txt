ARTIST: Son House

PROFILE:
Born: March 21, 1902, Riverton, Mississippi, USA
Died: October 19, 1988, Detroit, Michigan, USA

Son was an American blues singer and guitarist. House pioneered an innovative style featuring strong, repetitive rhythms, often played with the aid of slide guitar, and his singing often incorporated elements of southern gospel and spiritual music.
