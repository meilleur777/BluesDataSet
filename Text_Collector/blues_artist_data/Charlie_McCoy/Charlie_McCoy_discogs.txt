ARTIST: Charlie McCoy

PROFILE:
Grammy, Country Music Association and Academy of Country Music award-winning multi-instrumentalist who has also been a prolific Nashville session musician, especially on harmonica. Long-time music director for TV variety show [i]Hee Haw[/i]. 2008 inductee, West Virginia Music Hall of Fame.

Born March 28, 1941 in Oak Hill, West Virginia, USA.
