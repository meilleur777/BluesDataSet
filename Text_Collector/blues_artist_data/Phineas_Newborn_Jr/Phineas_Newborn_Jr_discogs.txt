ARTIST: Phineas Newborn Jr.

PROFILE:
American jazz pianist and composer.
Born December 14, 1931 in Whiteville, Hardeman County, Tennessee and died May 26, 1989 in Memphis, Tennessee.
He is the son of [a3843460] (drummer), the older brother of [a=Calvin Newborn] and has a son [a2391285] (actor).
