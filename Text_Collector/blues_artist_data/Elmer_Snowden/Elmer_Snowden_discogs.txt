ARTIST: Elmer Snowden

PROFILE:
American jazz banjoist, guitarist and bandleader, born October 9, 1900, Baltimore, Maryland, USA, died May 14, 1973, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA.
He was the original bandleader of [a370233] and worked with [a145257] and [a758216].
