ARTIST: Herman Brood

PROFILE:
Dutch rock 'n roll hero, painter, actor and lyricist, born 5 November 1946 in Zwolle, The Netherlands and died 11 July 2001 when he jumped from the roof of the Hilton-Hotel in Amsterdam, The Netherlands.
