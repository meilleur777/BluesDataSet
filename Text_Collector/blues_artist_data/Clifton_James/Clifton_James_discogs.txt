ARTIST: Clifton James

PROFILE:
American blues (and rhythm & blues) drummer.
Played with : Bo Diddley, Willie Dixon, Buddy Guy, Koko Taylor, Muddy Waters, Sonny Boy Williamson, Howlin' Wolf and others.

Born : October 02, 1936 in Chicago, Illinois.
Died : February 16, 2006 in Chicago, Illinois.
