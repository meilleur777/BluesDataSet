ARTIST: Stick McGhee

PROFILE:
American jump blues guitarist, singer and songwriter, best known for his blues song "Drinkin' Wine, Spo-Dee-O-Dee", which he wrote with [a=J. Mayo Williams].
Born: March 23, 1917 in Kingsport, Tennessee, USA
Died: August 15, 1961 in The Bronx, New York, USA (Lung cancer)

Younger brother of [a=Brownie McGhee].
