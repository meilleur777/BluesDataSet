ARTIST: Blues Image

PROFILE:
Rock band formed in Tampa, Florida, in 1966. They are best known for their 1970 hit, "Ride Captain Ride," which reached No. 4 on the Billboard Hot 100 and the Canadian RPM magazine charts.
