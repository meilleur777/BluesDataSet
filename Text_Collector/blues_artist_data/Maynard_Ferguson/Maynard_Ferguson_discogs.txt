ARTIST: Maynard Ferguson

PROFILE:
Canadian jazz trumpeter, and bandleader.

Born: 4 May 1928 in Verdun, Montreal, Canada.
Died: 23 August 2006 in Ventura, California, USA (aged 78).

Awarded the Order of Canada in 2005.
