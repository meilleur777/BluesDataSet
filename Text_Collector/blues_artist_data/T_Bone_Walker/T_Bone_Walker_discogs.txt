ARTIST: T-Bone Walker

PROFILE:
T-Bone Walker (born May 28, 1910, Linden, Texas, USA - died March 16, 1975, Los Angeles, California, USA) was an American blues guitarist, singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist. He began recording in 1929 and was one of the first artists to use an electric guitar in the 1930's. Perhaps his best known song is 1947's [i](They Call It) Stormy Monday[/i]. He was posthumously inducted into the [i]Rock And Roll Hall of Fame[/i] in 1987 (Early Influence).
