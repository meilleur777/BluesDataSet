ARTIST: Roomful Of Blues

PROFILE:
Roomful of Blues is an American blues and swing revival big band formed in Providence, Rhode Island, USA, in 1967.
With a recording career that spans over 50 years, they have toured worldwide and recorded many albums. Until 1979 the band was fronted by [a=Duke Robillard].
