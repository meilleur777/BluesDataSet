ARTIST: John Koerner

PROFILE:
American guitarist, singer, and songwriter best known as guitarist and vocalist for blues trio [a=Koerner, Ray & Glover]. Born August 31, 1938, Rochester, New York; lived in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Died May 18, 2024.
