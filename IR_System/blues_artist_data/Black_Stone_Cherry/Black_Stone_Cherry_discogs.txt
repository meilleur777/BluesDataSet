ARTIST: Black Stone Cherry

PROFILE:
Black Stone Cherry are a southern rock band formed on June 4, 2001 in Edmonton, Kentucky, USA. They are currently signed to Mascot Label Group. The band consists of Chris Robertson (vocals/guitar), Ben Wells (guitar/vocals), John Fred Young (drums/vocals).
