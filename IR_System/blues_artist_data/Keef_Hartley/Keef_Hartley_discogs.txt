ARTIST: Keef Hartley

PROFILE:
Keef Hartley (born 8 April 1944, Preston, Lancashire, England - died 26 November 2011, Fulwood, Preston, Lancashire, England) was an English drummer and percussionist who played with some of the greatest British blues rock musicians. He replaced [a=Ringo Starr] as drummer for [a=Rory Storm & The Hurricanes]. His own band [a305976] was the first British band to play at the [l506397]. He retired from the music industry in the mid-1970's.
