ARTIST: Elmore James

PROFILE:
American blues singer and guitarist, born in Holmes County, Mississippi, on January 27, 1918 and died in Chicago, Illinois, on May 24, 1963.
Inducted into Rock And Roll Hall of Fame in 1992 (early influence).
