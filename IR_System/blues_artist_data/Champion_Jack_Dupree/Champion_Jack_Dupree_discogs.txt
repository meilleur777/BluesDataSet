ARTIST: Champion Jack Dupree

PROFILE:
American pianist and singer, born 04 July 1910 (sometimes given as July 4, July 10, and July 23, in the years 1908, 1909, or 1910) in New Orleans, Louisiana, USA and died 21 January 1992 in Hannover, Germany. He enjoyed a long musical career after starting out as a boxer.
