ARTIST: Robert Lockwood Jr.

PROFILE:
American blues singer and guitarist, who first recorded for Bluebird in Chicago in 1941. Husband of [a4097888].

Born: March 27, 1915 Turkey Scratch, west of Helena, Arkansas.
Died: November 21, 2006 Cleveland, Ohio.
