ARTIST: Blues Creation

PROFILE:
Japanese rock band from Tokyo, active from 1969 to 1971.
They started off as standard late 60s traditional blues-rock band but quickly moved towards a sound steeped in heavy rock and acid-blues strongly guitar-driven. After their dissolution they changed their name to [a414055].
