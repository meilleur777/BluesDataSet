ARTIST: Nanker Phelge

PROFILE:
Nanker Phelge is a songwriter pseudonym used by [b][a=The Rolling Stones][/b] between 1963 and 1965. It was used for collaborations by the whole band, i.e. [a=Mick Jagger], [a=Brian Jones (5)], [a=Keith Richards], [a=Charlie Watts] and [a=Bill Wyman].
