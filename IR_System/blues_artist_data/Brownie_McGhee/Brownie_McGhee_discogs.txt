ARTIST: Brownie McGhee

PROFILE:
American folk music and Piedmont blues singer and guitarist, best known for his collaboration with the harmonica player [a=Sonny Terry].
Born: November 30, 1915 in Knoxville, Tennessee.
Died: February 16, 1996 in Oakland, California.

Brother of [a=Stick McGhee].
