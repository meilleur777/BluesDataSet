ARTIST: Hubert Félix Thiéfaine

PROFILE:
Hubert-Félix Thiéfaine is a French singer-songwriter born on July 21st, 1948 to Dole (Jura). Double prize-winner at the French " Victoires de la Musique" in 2012, he was also entitled "Commandeur des Arts et des Lettres".
His musical career of forty years makes of him one of the figures of the French song.
His literary, musical, film and historic culture allows him to play with diverse references.
Thiéfaine does not hesitate to give to his texts an enigmatic size dimension.
