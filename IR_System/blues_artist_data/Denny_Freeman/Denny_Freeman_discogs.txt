ARTIST: Denny Freeman

PROFILE:
American Texas and electric blues guitarist, born August 7, 1944 in Orlando, Florida. Died April 25, 2021.
Although he was primarily known as a guitar player, Freeman also played piano and electric organ, both in concert and on record.
