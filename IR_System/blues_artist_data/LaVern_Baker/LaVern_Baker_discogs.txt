ARTIST: LaVern Baker

PROFILE:
American R&B singer who had several hit records on the pop chart in the 1950s and early 1960s.
Born: Delores Williams on November 11, 1929 in Chicago, IL
Died: March 10, 1997 in New York City, NY
Inducted into Rock And Roll Hall of Fame in 1991 (Performer).
