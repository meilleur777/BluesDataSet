ARTIST: Rod Stewart

PROFILE:
British singer, born January 10, 1945 in London, England. In addition to his successful solo career, he has also been a member of successful high-profile rock groups the [a=Jeff Beck Group] and [url=http://www.discogs.com/artist/289040-Faces-3]the Faces[/url].

Inducted into Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1994 as a solo artist, and again in 2012 as a member of the Faces (both as a Performer).
